    template inheritence (jinja m ):- 
        Template inheritance Jinja2 ka ek mahatvapurn feature hai,
         jo aapki HTML code ko adhik sangathit aur prabandhaniya bana sakta hai.
         Iska mukhya uddeshya code ke punah prayog ko badhava dena hai.
         Udaharan ke liye, maan lijiye ki aapke paas 100 pages hain aur
         har page mein ek hi tarah ka navbar hai. Agar aapko navbar mein
         koi chhota sa badlav karna ho, to aapko 100 files mein ja kar ise
         badalne ki zarurat nahi hogi. Template inheritance ki madad se aap aisa kar sakte hain.

         Template Inheritance kya hai?
         Template inheritance ka matlab hai ki aap ek base template banate
         hain jismein sabhi samanay HTML structure hote hain, jaise ki navbar,
         header, footer, etc. Phir aap anya templates mein is base template
         ko inherit karte hain aur keval ve hise badalte hain jo alag hote hain.
